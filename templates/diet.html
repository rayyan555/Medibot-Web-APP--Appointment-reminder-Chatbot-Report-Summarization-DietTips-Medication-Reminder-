Diet & Nutrition Guide

Why Diet is Important?

Nutrition plays a vital role in maintaining health and preventing disease. A balanced diet strengthens immunity, maintains weight, and enhances your overall wellbeing. Your food choices impact your energy levels, mental clarity, and recovery process.

Top 5 Healthy Eating Tips

  • 🧃 Stay hydrated – Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • 🥗 Include more fruits, vegetables, and fiber-rich foods in your meals.
  • 🕐 Eat on time – Maintain regular meal timings to keep metabolism stable.
  • 🍳 Choose lean protein – Eggs, chicken breast, tofu, legumes are great sources.
  • 🧂 Reduce salt & sugar intake – Avoid processed snacks and sugary beverages.

Macronutrient Breakdown

🍞 Carbohydrates

Primary source of energy. Focus on whole grains, oats, and fruits.

🍗 Proteins

Essential for muscle repair. Good sources include lean meat, eggs, and legumes.

🥑 Fats

Supports brain function. Prefer healthy fats like olive oil, nuts, and seeds.

🍽️ Sample Daily Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Oats with banana, almonds, and honey + green tea
  • Lunch: Brown rice, grilled chicken, mixed vegetables
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with berries
  • Dinner: Lentil soup, salad, multigrain toast

🧠 Brain-Boosting Foods

🥦 Broccoli – packed with antioxidants and vitamin K
🐟 Salmon – omega-3 fatty acids improve cognitive health
🍫 Dark Chocolate – boosts mood and focus (in moderation)
🍓 Berries – rich in flavonoids for memory support
